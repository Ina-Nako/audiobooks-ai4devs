# Project Brief — AudioBooks

## What we are building

AudioBooks is a full-featured audiobook platform with an audio-first, accessible interface. Users can **discover and listen to audiobooks**, **buy printed copies**, **explore book-related podcasts**, and **participate in a community forum** with other readers and listeners.

The platform is designed to be the one-stop destination for book lovers — whether they prefer listening, reading, or discussing.

## Who it is for

- **Casual listeners** — people who want a simple, fast way to find and play audiobooks
- **Avid readers** — book enthusiasts who want to listen AND buy printed copies
- **Podcast fans** — users who enjoy book-related podcasts (author interviews, book discussions, literary analysis)
- **Community members** — readers who want to discuss, review, and recommend books to others
- **Accessibility-first users** — blind users and people with dyslexia (works well with screen readers and keyboard navigation)

## What users can do

### Audiobooks
- Search and browse a catalog of audiobooks with filters (genre, language, duration, author)
- Play audiobooks with full player controls (pause/resume, speed control, progress tracking)
- Use "Quick Listen" for shorter, summarized versions of books
- See similar titles with explanations of why they're recommended
- Rate and review audiobooks

### Bookstore
- Browse printed copies of books available in the catalog
- Add books to a shopping cart and complete purchases
- Track orders and view purchase history

### Podcasts
- Discover book-related podcasts (author interviews, book clubs, literary analysis)
- Browse podcast shows and individual episodes
- Listen to podcast episodes with the built-in player
- Subscribe to shows and manage a personal podcast queue

### Community Forum
- Create discussion threads about audiobooks, printed books, or podcasts
- Reply to threads, upvote posts, and engage with other community members
- Browse discussions by category (genres, recommendations, reviews, general)
- Search across all forum content

### User Accounts
- Sign up with email and password
- Log in and manage a personal profile
- Maintain a personal library (listening history, wishlist, purchases)
- Receive notifications (forum replies, new podcast episodes, order updates)

## Main sections

| Section | Key screens |
|---------|-------------|
| **Home** | Featured audiobooks, trending forum posts, latest podcast episodes, personalized recommendations |
| **Catalog** | Browse, search, and filter audiobooks |
| **Book Details** | Synopsis, narrator info, play, Quick Listen, similar titles, reviews, buy printed copy |
| **Player** | Playback controls, speed, progress |
| **Bookstore** | Printed book listings, shopping cart, checkout, order history |
| **Podcast Hub** | Browse shows, episode lists, podcast player |
| **Forum** | Thread list, thread detail, create thread, user posts |
| **Profile** | Library, listening history, wishlist, purchases, settings |
| **Auth** | Login, sign up, password reset |
