# Project Brief - AudioBooks

## What we are building
iCan is an audiobook app with an audio-first, accessible interface. People should be able to find audiobooks, start listening quickly, and browse the catalog on any device.

## Who it is for
- Blind users and people with dyslexia (works well with screen readers and keyboard navigation)
- Busy listeners who want a fast, simple listening experience

## What users can do
- Search and browse audiobooks, then open a book to see details
- Play audiobooks (pause/resume, change speed)
- Use accessibility options (keyboard navigation, screen-reader-friendly labels)
- Use "Quick Listen" for shorter, summarized versions
- See similar titles with reasons

## Main screens
- Catalog: browse, search, and filter audiobooks
- Book details: synopsis, narrator info, play, Quick Listen, similar titles
- Player: playback controls and speed
- Quick Listen: summary with estimated time

## Future additions (not in MVP)
- User accounts, sign in, and library management
- Bookmarks and voice notes
- Admin moderation and creator tools
- Home screen with recommendations and "continue listening"
- Forum to disccus different books
- Buying a book in printed form
